Rules for the Kozers Universe:

  1. No genuine toxicity towards anyone, no matter the situation.
  2. No cheating, hacking, lag machines, ban evasion, and only approved clients.
    • Allowed clients; Lunar, Badlion, Optifine, Iris.
    • Clients allowed only with select mods; Fabric and Forge.
    • Disallowed Clients; Meteor, Impact, Baritone, Wurst, Lambda, Future, Konas, and etc...
    • Lag machines include : gigantic pens of animals, large meaningless redstone contraptions, and etc...
    • Ban evasion is logging on to the server with a different account in any manner, whether from the same IP or a different one.
    • Litematica is no longer allowed, since we hold building competions with prizes.
  3. No bullying / trolling. Things like ;
    • Harassment of any kind, no matter the situation.
    • No griefing, things like needlessly blowing up neighboring land, building walls that border / obstruct a claim, or making land claims that surround another claim.
    • No forcing people into a specific roleplay scenario.
    • No trapping people unless they are outlaws or it falls under agreed terms of war.
    • Outlaws can only be trapped in dedicated prisons using the towny plot type.
    • No disrespecting staff for any reason. If you believe a staff member is being disrespectful themselves, make a support ticketand report it.
  4. Exploitation of bugs is basis for punishment.
    • We recommend reporting bugs over exploiting, to benefit everyone.
    • Severity and usage of exploit changes punishment / reward.
  5. Combat logging is disallowed during wars. You will recieve punishment for doing so.
  6. No bribing staff for any reason. You will receive punishment for doing so.
  7. Vulgar use of language is not allowed.
    • Over use of swearing is basis for punishment.
    • Use of derogatory language is not taken lightly and will result in a much more severe punishments.
      • What language is considered vulgar is up to staff's discretion but can be discussed.
  8. You may not play on an alternative or alt account.
    • If discovered, the account with the least playtime will be banned.
  9. Discussion of religion and politics is allowed, as long as it is in good faith.
    • Proselytization or perpetuation of political views is not allowed.
  10. Chat rules are as follows:
    • The primary language of the server is english.
      • There are no restrictions on what language is used in towns, signs, or named items.
      • Try to keep chat in english for sake of ease of communication and readability.
    • Spamming is not allowed.

NOTE: Roleplay doesnt make you exempt from any rules
